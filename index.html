Lighting The Way ABA Therapy

In-Home Early Intervention Services

“Education is the movement from darkness to light.” – Allan Bloom

Our Mission: Using the principles of Applied Behavior Analysis to teach our early learners the necessary skills that will help them to achieve their full potential and improve their quality of life.

Therapy in the home environment enables us to have greater involvement of the caretakers, and also it allows us to see how children function in the place they spends most of their time. It also helps many families who find it easier to have the therapists come to them, rather than planning the time and transportation to another facility.

Now serving Duval, Saint Johns, and Clay County
